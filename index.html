Welcome to the Science and Technology Innovation Club (STIC) at MediCaps University, Indore! We are a dynamic community dedicated to fostering innovation and collaboration among students passionate about science and technology

About STIC

STIC brings together students from various disciplines to explore and engage in STEM fields. Through workshops, hackathons, and research projects, we promote hands-on learning and skill enhancement. Whether you're interested in coding, robotics, or biotechnology, STIC is the ideal place to connect, learn, and innovate. Join us in our journey to make a meaningful impact through science and technology!
